FAQs

How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Send an email with a brief description and screenshots (if possible) to bkirby1967@gmail.com.
Where is my data stored?
Data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced using your personal iCloud account. We do not operate servers that store your content.
How do I request data deletion?
Because we do not collect your data, there is no server-side data to delete. You can remove the app’s local data by deleting the app from your device, and you can remove iCloud data via iOS Settings → your name → iCloud → iCloud Drive.
