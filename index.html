What Travel Sage helps you do

Travel Sage is designed for travelers who like to stay organized but still be flexible. Plan trips, explore locations, and keep your photos and memories attached to the places you actually visited.

Plan trips with clear structure

Create trips, add locations, and keep the important details together: cities, landmarks, hotels, and more.

Add new trips and define when and where you're going.

Search for places you care about

Use integrated search controls to find addresses, points of interest, and locations near where you plan to stay.

Focused search tools help you find exactly what you need.

See your trip on a map

Switch to a map view to see every location laid out geographically, so you can optimize routes and time.

Tap a location to see details, notes, and nearby points of interest.

Plan the route to the next location

Plan your next move and see the best path from one stop to the next. Travel Sage helps you make smart decisions about timing and distance.

Follow the path between locations so you’re never surprised by how far your next stop is.

Work from a location list

Keep a structured list of all your trip locations so you can jump between stops, check them off as you go, and see what’s coming up next.

A clean list view keeps your cities, hotels, and points of interest organized and easy to navigate.

See notes and pictures for each location

Open any location to see notes and photos you've saved. Travel Sage keeps everything organized, so you can revisit memories anytime.

Keep important details alongside the photos you took there.

Match your photos to places

Browse your photo library, select images, and save them to the album. They will be automatically associated to each location.

Choose photos that match where you've been and keep them organized.

Share trips privately via iCloud

When you choose to share, Travel Sage uses iCloud sharing so your trip and photos stay within your trusted circle.

Share selected trips with family or friends while keeping control.
